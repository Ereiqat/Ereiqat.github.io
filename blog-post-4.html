Kimo Fall - Hyperfall Cube Game

Kimo Fall is an exhilarating hyperfall cube game that challenges players to test their reflexes and agility. As the protagonist, a red cube, your objective is to survive as long as possible while avoiding deadly spikes and navigating through an array of challenging obstacles.

Kimo fall is the hardest game I played in my entire life.
Mamoon Sadeddin

Objectives:

The main goal is to achieve the highest score by skillfully navigating the cube through a series of falling platforms and perilous spikes. Survive as long as possible to secure your position on the global leaderboard.

development

Kimo Fall was developed using Unity game engine, and the game mechanics were carefully crafted to provide a thrilling and challenging experience. The team faced various obstacles during development, such as optimizing performance and refining the difficulty curve to strike the perfect balance between enjoyment and challenge.
