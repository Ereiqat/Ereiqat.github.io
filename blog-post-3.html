PSUT Fire Fighter Robotics Competition - First Place Victory

In Sep, 2022, I had the incredible opportunity to participate in the highly anticipated PSUT Fire Fighter robotics competition held at Princess Sumaya University for Technology (PSUT). As part of a talented and dedicated team, we named ourselves Double Face, and I took on the role of Team Leader. Our collective efforts, along with my individual contributions, led us to a resounding victory in the competition.

The PSUT Fire Fighter robotics competition was an unforgettable experience that showcased our skills, determination, and passion for robotics. As we continue our journey in this exciting field, we look forward to exploring new challenges and making meaningful contributions to the world of robotics.
Ahmad Al-Omari

Our team, Double Face, was comprised of passionate robotics enthusiasts who shared a common goal: to design and build a robot that would excel in the simulated firefighting challenges.

Robot Features:

  • Can quickly manouver in tight places to collect balls.
  • Has the ability to empty the tanks in such a short time.
  • The ability to save more than one animal simultaneously

Competition Success:

At the 2022 PSUT Fire fighter Competition, our robot performed exceptionally great, securing the 1st place award among a talented pool of competitors. The robot's ability to save animals and collecting the balls was key to its success.

Acknowledgments:

Our achievement would not have been possible without the support and guidance of various individuals and organizations. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to:

  • Eng. Youseg Jarbou & Eng. Omar Tobassi for their guidance and mentorship.
